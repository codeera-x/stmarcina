Why Vaccinations matter

Vaccines protect children from deadly diseases like measles, diphtheria, and polio.
Early vaccination helps build strong immunity for life.

  • Saves millions of lives yearly
  • Recommended by WHO & NCDC
  • Safe, effective, and free at our center
